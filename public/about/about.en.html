🃏 How to Play FreeCell

Your goal: move all 52 cards into four foundation piles (one for each suit), starting with the Ace and ending with the King.

1. Set Up the Table

  • Deal eight piles of face-up cards (the tableau).
  • Four empty free cells appear in the top left.
  • Four empty foundation slots sit in the top right.

2. Your Moves

  • Move a card into a free cell to save it for later.
  • Stack cards only if they’re one rank lower and the opposite color.
  • Send cards to their foundation as soon as possible.

3. Use Free Cells Wisely

The four free cells are your secret helpers—don’t fill them all at once.

4. Build Your Foundations

Start with the Aces and add cards in order up to the King.

5. Winning the Game 🏆

When all 52 cards are stacked neatly from Ace through King in their foundations, you win!

🎮 Controls

Playing is simple:

Mouse or Touch: Click or tap a card, then drag it into an empty space or onto one of the four home piles.

Keyboard: Use arrow keys to move between cards, Space to send a valid card to a home pile.

Quick Restart: Press "R" for a new shuffle.

💡 Tips and Tricks for Freecell

1. Use Empty Columns

Clearing a column gives you extra space to move stacks around.

2. Free Your Low Cards Early

Aces and Twos should go to the foundation quickly to unlock moves.

3. Treasure Boxes

Use free cells to store cards temporarily, but don’t fill them all.

4. Plan Two Moves Ahead

Think before moving—avoid blocking important cards.

5. Bounce Back Anytime

Your game is saved, so you can continue anytime.
